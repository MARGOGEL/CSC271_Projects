Partnerships

Who are our partners?

Homely partners with banks and financial institutions of all sizes to give homebuyers access to an online mortgage marketplace. Our platform allows homebuyers to apply for mortgages and compare offers from various lenders using a marketplace bidding system. We allow mortgage loan officers at competing banks to view each mortgage application, negotiate, and offer their best terms to the prospective homebuyer. While we do partner with larger banks, we hope to forge more partnerships with many local banks to create a system of community benefit.

Cost of Platform for Lenders

Small Banks Small banks can use our platform at a monthly subscription price of $250 USD.
Medium Banks Medium banks may pay a flat-rate fee of 0.08% of the price of each home mortgage.
Large Banks Large banks may pay a flat-rate fee of 0.05% of the price of each home mortgage.
